Curriculum

Two Real Health AI Problems

Both problems use real datasets. Both demand real engineering. Both teach you how researchers think.

🧬
Problem 1 — DNA Sequence Classification
Kaggle · Multi-species Gene Family Dataset
  • 1.1EDA — Load & Explore DNA Data
  • 1.2Feature Engineering — k-mer Frequency Extraction
  • 1.3Dimensionality Reduction — PCA & UMAP
  • 1.4Feature Selection — LASSO (L1 Regularization)
  • 1.5Tabular ML — XGBoost (Main Model)
  • 1.6Model Comparison — LightGBM · CatBoost · RF
  • 1.7Interpretability — SHAP Values
  • 1.8Scoreboard & Evaluation Summary
🫀
Problem 2 — Heart Attack Risk Prediction
UCI · Clinical Cardiovascular Dataset
  • 2.1EDA — Load & Explore Heart Attack Data
  • 2.2Class Imbalance — Class Weights & SMOTE
  • 2.3Feature Selection — LASSO & Elastic Net
  • 2.4Tabular ML — XGBoost Tuning & Early Stopping
  • 2.5Model Comparison Scoreboard
  • 2.6Interpretability — SHAP + Permutation Importance
  • 2.7Survival Analysis — Cox Proportional Hazards
  • 2.8Final Scoreboard & Clinical Insights
Clinical Disclaimer: These are educational models built for learning. They are not intended for real clinical decision-making and require proper clinical validation before any medical use.
